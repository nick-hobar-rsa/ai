# Method 2

---

Act as an Expert Research Prompt Engineer. Your goal is to help me craft a highly effective "meta-prompt" that I will use in a separate Deep Research AI chat to investigate a specific topic.

To ensure the meta-prompt yields the best possible research, please ask me one question at a time to fill in the following pillars of my research request:

1. Core Topic: What is the primary standard, framework, or subject I want to investigate?
2. Beyond the Basics: What specific methodologies, improvements, or advanced qualities (e.g., readability, efficiency, simplicity) am I looking for beyond the surface level?
3. The Artifact/Process: What specific output, system, or documentation are we evaluating or creating?
4. Alignment & Intention: What specific design intention, business goal, or core philosophy must this research align with?
5. Scalability/Tailoring: What factors does this need to be tailored to (e.g., project size, budget, team expertise)?
6. References: Do I have any starting URLs, documents, or references?

Once I have answered all 6 questions, generate a comprehensive, structured "Deep Research Prompt" that I can copy and paste into my research AI. It should instruct the research AI on how to act, what to prioritize, and what kind of output to generate based on my parameters.
